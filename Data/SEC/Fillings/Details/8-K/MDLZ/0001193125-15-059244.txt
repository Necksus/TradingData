</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Mondel&#275;z International Announces Roberto Marques </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>to Become EVP and President, North America </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>J&amp;J Veteran Brings Extensive Global CPG Leadership Experience to Role </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">DEERFIELD, IL &#150; Feb. 24, 2015 &#150; Mondel&#275;z International, the world&#146;s pre-eminent maker of biscuits, chocolate, gum and
candy, today announced that Roberto Marques, 49, will join the company in mid-March and become Executive Vice President and President, North America. Marques will report to Chairman and CEO Irene Rosenfeld and be based in East Hanover, N.J. In this
role, he will be responsible for leading the company&#146;s $7 billion business in the United States and Canada, which includes Power Brands like <I>Oreo</I> and <I>belVita</I> biscuits; <I>Triscuit</I> and <I>Wheat Thins</I> crackers, <I>Halls</I>
candy and <I>Trident</I> gum. Marques succeeds Mark Clouse, who became the company&#146;s Chief Growth Officer in July 2014. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;I&#146;m delighted to welcome Roberto to the Mondel&#275;z International family,&#148; said Rosenfeld. &#147;His knowledge of consumer
brands, strong leadership capabilities and extensive global experience make him a terrific addition to our North America region and my leadership team. He will play a critical role in driving our growth and margin-expansion agendas.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In this role, Marques will also become a business sponsor on the company&#146;s Supply Chain Reinvention Steering Committee and
Sales&nbsp;&amp; Marketing Optimization team as well as a member of Mondel&#275;z International&#146;s newly formed Growth Council. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Marques joins Mondel&#275;z International after a successful career at Johnson&nbsp;&amp; Johnson, where he most recently served as Company
Group Chairman, Consumer North America, overseeing all aspects the $6 billion business, including beauty, baby and personal products and over-the-counter medicines. Prior to that, he held several senior management roles at J&amp;J, including Company
Group Chairman Consumer Health Care Global Business Unit, Worldwide President for the Vision Care Division, and President of Europe, Africa, Middle East and Latin America for Vision Care. </P>
 <p STYLE="margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt ; font-size:8pt">&nbsp;</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center">1 </P>

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">His track record includes driving growth through global platforms and leading multiple businesses
and functions, including start-ups, turnarounds, effective repositioning and growth of leading household brands, including <I>Acuvue, Aveeno, Band-Aid, Johnson&#146;s, Listerine, Neutrogena, Neosporin, Rogaine, Splenda, Tylenol</I> and
<I>Zyrtec</I>. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;I&#146;m thrilled to help Mondel&#275;z International grow its portfolio of iconic brands,&#148; Marques said.
&#147;I look forward to partnering with Irene and the rest of her leadership team to shape the future of the North American business and of Mondel&#275;z International.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Marques is a board member of the Consumer Health Care Products Association, ENACTUS and the Brazil-U.S. Business Council at the U.S. Chamber
