<p align="center" style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Kraft Recommends
Rejection of Mini-Tender Offer by TRC Capital Corporation</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">NORTHFIELD, Ill., April
23, 2007 &#151; Kraft Foods Inc. (NYSE: KFT) has been notified of an unsolicited &#147;mini-tender
offer&#148; by TRC Capital Corporation of Toronto (TRC) for TRC to purchase in cash
up to three million shares, or less than 0.2% percent of the outstanding Kraft
common stock, at a price of $31.00 per share. TRC&#146;s offer price of $31.00 per
share represents a 6.3% discount to the April 20, 2007 closing price of $33.09
per share on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE). Kraft does not endorse TRC&#146;s
unsolicited mini-tender offer and recommends that stockholders not tender their
shares in response to this mini-tender offer. Kraft is not in any way
associated with TRC, this mini-tender offer or the offer documentation.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mini-tender offers, such
as this one by TRC, do not give investors the same level of protection afforded
by larger tender offers.&#160; For example, in
making this offer, TRC is not required to file disclosure and other offer
documents with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) or adhere to
additional procedures mandated by U.S. securities laws.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Kraft urges investors to:</font></p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10.0pt;margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font>&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Obtain current market quotes for
their shares of common stock,</p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10.0pt;margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font>&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Consult with their financial
advisors, and</p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10.0pt;margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font>&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Exercise caution with respect to
TRC&#146;s offer.</p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The SEC has issued &#147;Investor
Tips&#148; on mini-tender offers, which note that often in making the offers at
below-market prices, &#147;bidders are hoping that they will catch investors off
guard if the investors do not compare the offer price to the current market
price.&#148;. The SEC&#146;s advisory is available at
www.sec.gov/investor/pubs/minitend.htm.</font></p>
