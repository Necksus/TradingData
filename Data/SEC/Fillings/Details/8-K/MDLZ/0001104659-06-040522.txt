<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item&nbsp;5.02.
Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors;
Appointment of Principal Officers.</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On June&nbsp;5,
2006, Kraft Foods Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) appointed Pamela King as the Company&#146;s
Senior Vice President and Corporate Controller, in which capacity she will be
the Company&#146;s principal accounting officer. Ms.&nbsp;King replaces David
Brearton, who was named Executive Vice President, Global Business Services on June&nbsp;5,
2006 and remains with the Company.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Ms.&nbsp;King, 49,
has been with Kraft since September&nbsp;1983 and, since January&nbsp;2005, has
served as Vice President Finance of the Company&#146;s Canadian operations. Prior
thereto, Ms.&nbsp;King served as the Company&#146;s Vice President Finance for North
America Sales and Customer Service from May&nbsp;2001 to January&nbsp;2005 and
as Controller Procurement from September&nbsp;2000 to May&nbsp;2001.</font></p>
