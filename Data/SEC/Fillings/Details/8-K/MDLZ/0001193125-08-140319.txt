</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>KRAFT FOODS COMMENCES SPLIT-OFF EXCHANGE OFFER </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>FOR THE </B><B><I>POST </I></B><B>CEREALS BUSINESS </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT
FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><I>- 10% Exchange Offer Discount Announced - </I></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><I>- Transaction Expected to Close August&nbsp;4, 2008 -
</I></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">NORTHFIELD, Ill.&#151; June&nbsp;25, 2008 &#151; Kraft Foods Inc. (NYSE: KFT) today announced that it has commenced its exchange offer
related to the split-off transaction of its <I>Post</I> cereals business. The split-off transaction is in connection with the merger of Cable Holdco, Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of Kraft that will own certain assets and liabilities of the
<I>Post</I> cereals business, and a subsidiary of Ralcorp Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: RAH). As previously announced, Kraft entered into a definitive agreement on November&nbsp;15, 2007, to distribute and merge its <I>Post</I> cereals business into
Ralcorp. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">In this split-off transaction, Kraft shareholders will have the option to exchange some or all of their shares of Kraft common
