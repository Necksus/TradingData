ALIGN="center"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><I>Exchange Offer Fully Subscribed and Subject to Pro-Ration </I></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">NORTHFIELD,
Ill.&#151;August 4, 2008 &#151; Kraft Foods Inc. (NYSE: KFT) today announced the preliminary results of its exchange offer related to the split-off of its <I>Post</I> cereals business and the closing of the merger of its <I>Post</I> cereals business
into a subsidiary of Ralcorp Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: RAH). </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Based on preliminary estimates of Kraft shares validly tendered and not
withdrawn, the exchange offer will be subject to a proration factor of approximately 7.85%. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">In this split-off transaction, Kraft
shareholders had the option to exchange their shares of Kraft common stock for shares of Cable Holdco, Inc., which prior to the merger with a Ralcorp subsidiary, was a wholly owned subsidiary of Kraft that owned certain assets and liabilities of the
<I>Post</I> cereals business. With the completion of the merger of Cable Holdco and a Ralcorp subsidiary, the Cable Holdco common stock was exchanged for shares of Ralcorp common stock on a one-for-one basis. As a result, Kraft shareholders that
tendered their Kraft shares as part of the exchange offer now own 0.6606 shares of Ralcorp for each Kraft share exchanged. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exchange Offer Results </B>
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Pursuant to the exchange offer, which expired at 8:00 a.m., New York City time, this morning, Kraft announced that it has accepted
46,119,899 common shares of Kraft for 30,466,805 shares of Cable Holdco. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The exchange agent preliminarily estimates that a total of
581,326,305 Kraft common shares were validly tendered and not withdrawn prior to the expiration of the exchange offer, including 427,128,323 shares tendered pursuant to guaranteed delivery procedures. The total shares tendered include an estimated
533,776 Kraft shares tendered by odd lot shareholders not subject to proration. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>
