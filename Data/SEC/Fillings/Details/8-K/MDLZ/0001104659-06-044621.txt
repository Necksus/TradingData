<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item&nbsp;1.01.&nbsp;&nbsp; Entry
into a Material Definitive Agreement.</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">See the discussion set

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item&nbsp;5.02.&nbsp;&nbsp; Departure
of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of
Principal Officers.</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(b)&nbsp;On June&nbsp;26,
2006, Kraft Foods Inc. (&#147;Kraft&#148;) and Mr.&nbsp;Roger K. Deromedi, Chief
Executive Officer of Kraft, announced that Mr.&nbsp;Deromedi had mutually
agreed with the Board of Directors of Kraft (the &#147;Board&#148;) to leave Kraft to
pursue other interests.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(c)(1)&nbsp;On June&nbsp;26,
2006, Kraft announced the appointment of Irene B. Rosenfeld as Chief Executive
Officer and a director of Kraft, effective immediately.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(2)&nbsp; Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld,
age 53, served as Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Frito-Lay,&nbsp;Inc.,
a division of PepsiCo,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;PepsiCo&#148;), from September&nbsp;2004 until June&nbsp;2006.
Prior to joining PepsiCo, Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld worked for more than 20 years at
Kraft.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(3)&nbsp; Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld&#146;s
annual base salary is $1,300,000 for 2006. In February&nbsp;2007, she will
receive a guaranteed bonus of $1,950,000 under the Annual Incentive Plan and an
award of $3,250,000 under the Long Term Incentive Plan. Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld is
also eligible to receive annual restricted stock awards subject to a three-year
vesting period. In January&nbsp;2007, she will receive an award in a range
between $3,120,000 and $8,850,000.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld was
awarded 387,230 restricted shares of Kraft Foods Class&nbsp;A Common Stock on June&nbsp;27,
2006 as a sign-on bonus in recognition of equity awards that she forfeited upon
leaving her previous position. This restricted stock award vests as
follows:&#160; 161,346 shares on July&nbsp;1,
2009 and 225,884 shares on July&nbsp;1, 2011. Under certain conditions the
vesting would be accelerated. In particular, vesting would be accelerated
should Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld not be appointed Chairman and Chief Executive Officer
prior to January&nbsp;1, 2008, or if a person other than Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld is
appointed to replace the current Chairman.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld&#146;s
pension benefits will be calculated as if she had been employed continuously by
the Company since 1981.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(d)&nbsp; As stated
above, on June&nbsp;26, 2006 the Board announced the appointment of Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld
as Chief Executive Officer and a director of Kraft, effective immediately.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On June&nbsp;26, 2006,
Kraft issued a press release announcing Mr.&nbsp;Deromedi&#146;s departure and Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld&#146;s
appointment. A copy of the press release, which is dated June&nbsp;26, 2006, is
attached as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1 to this Current Report on Form&nbsp;8-K and
is incorporated herein by reference.</font></p>

<p align="center" style="color:windowtext;margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-align:center;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">IRENE B.
ROSENFELD NAMED<br>
CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER OF KRAFT FOODS INC.</font></u></b></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">NORTHFIELD, IL &#151; June 26, 2006 &#151; The Board of
Directors of Kraft Foods Inc. (NYSE:KFT) today announced the appointment of
Irene&nbsp;B. Rosenfeld as Chief Executive Officer, effective immediately,
replacing Roger&nbsp;K. Deromedi, who has mutually agreed with the Board to
leave the Company to pursue other interests. Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld was most
recently Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Frito-Lay, a division of
PepsiCo.</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;The entire Board is delighted to have Irene in place.
While the fundamentals of the business continue to improve, we are confident
that Irene will accelerate the execution of Kraft&#146;s growth strategy, build
value for shareholders, and lead Kraft when it becomes a fully independent
company,&#148; said Louis&nbsp;C. Camilleri, Chairman of the Board of Kraft Foods.</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr. Camilleri said, &#147;Irene is one of the most talented
and respected executives in the consumer goods industry and we are thrilled
that she is coming home to Kraft. She has repeatedly demonstrated her ability
to drive innovation throughout her career. Her decisiveness, personal warmth,
and emotional intelligence are invaluable assets. Irene has a wealth of
experience in all of Kraft&#146;s businesses and is uniquely qualified to lead Kraft
during these challenging and exciting times.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;I am deeply honored to lead Kraft in the years
ahead,&#148; said Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld. &#147;I genuinely love this Company, its brands,
its people, and its values. I believe that we have a very bright future ahead
of us and look forward to working with the Board and our management team to
assure Kraft&#146;s leadership in the food and beverage industry.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Prior to joining Frito-Lay in 2004, Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld
spent more than 20 years with Kraft and General Foods. She held a number of key
management positions in the United States, Canada and Mexico, culminating in
her appointment as President of Kraft&#146;s North American business. Among her many
accomplishments at Kraft, Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld led the highly successful
integration of the $19&nbsp;billion Nabisco acquisition as well as the
