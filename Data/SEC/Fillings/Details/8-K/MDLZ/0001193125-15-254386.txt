</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:18pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Mondel&#275;z International Completes Acquisition of </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:18pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Majority Stake in Vietnam&#146;s Leading Snacks Business </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Strengthens Company&#146;s Core Snacking Categories in Dynamic, High-Growth Market </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">DEERFIELD, Ill. &#150; July&nbsp;16, 2015 &#150; Mondel&#275;z International today announced it has completed the acquisition of an 80 percent
stake in Kinh Do, Vietnam&#146;s leading snacks business. First announced in November 2014, the combination brings together Kinh Do&#146;s well-loved local snacks, including <I>Kinh Do</I> mooncakes and biscuits, <I>Cosy</I> biscuits, <I>Solite</I>
soft cakes and <I>AFC </I>crackers, with Mondel&#275;z International&#146;s iconic global brands, such as <I>Oreo</I> cookies, <I>Ritz</I> crackers and <I>Cadbury</I> chocolate. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;This acquisition is a perfect fit for our growth strategy in Asia Pacific, strengthening our core snacking categories in a high-growth
dynamic market,&#148; said Tim Cofer, Executive Vice President and President, Asia Pacific and Eastern Europe, Middle East&nbsp;&amp; Africa. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Vietnam is home to over 90&nbsp;million increasingly sophisticated consumers looking for high-quality snacking products. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;The addition of Kinh Do&#146;s deep understanding of Vietnam&#146;s consumers and routes to market enhances our capabilities in the
region,&#148; Cofer said. &#147;Combined with our global knowledge and resources, including world-class innovation, marketing, food safety and quality, the future is bright for our business in Vietnam.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>About Mondel&#275;z International </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mondel&#275;z International, Inc. (NASDAQ: MDLZ) is a global snacking powerhouse, with pro forma 2014 revenue of more than $30
billion.&nbsp;Creating delicious moments of joy in 165 countries, Mondel&#275;z International is a world leader in biscuits, chocolate, gum, candy and powdered beverages, with billion-dollar brands such as <I>Oreo</I>, <I>LU</I> and <I>Nabisco</I>
biscuits; <I>Cadbury, Cadbury Dairy Milk </I>and <I>Milka</I> chocolate; <I>Trident</I> gum and <I>Tang</I> powdered beverages.&nbsp;Mondel&#275;z International is a proud member of the Standard and Poor&#146;s 500, NASDAQ 100 and Dow Jones
Sustainability Index. Visit <U>www.mondelezinternational.com</U> or follow us on Twitter at <U>www.twitter.com/MDLZ</U>. </P>
