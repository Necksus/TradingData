<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item&nbsp;1.01.
&#160;Entry into a Material Definitive
Agreement.</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On May 24, 2006 (the &#147;Transaction
Date&#148;), Kraft Foods Global, Inc. (&#147;Kraft&#148;), a wholly owned subsidiary of Kraft
Foods Inc., entered into several agreements with Altria Corporate Services,
Inc. (&#147;ALCS&#148;), pursuant to which Kraft either purchased from ALCS, or assumed
ALCS&#146;s obligations relating to the lease of, certain real property, as
described below. <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">&#160;</font></b>The
real property involved in these transactions, though owned or leased by ALCS,
was being used by Kraft as part of its operations, for which use Kraft had been
reimbursing ALCS.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Wilkes Barre,
Pennsylvania.</font></i>&#160; On the Transaction Date, Kraft entered into a
Purchase and Sale Agreement (the &#147;Purchase Agreement&#148;) with ALCS, pursuant to
which Kraft purchased certain real estate located in Wilkes Barre, Pennsylvania
(the &#147;Wilkes Barre Real Property&#148;) from ALCS.&#160;
In addition, pursuant to the Purchase Agreement, ALCS sold to Kraft, and
Kraft purchased from ALCS, certain personal property located on the Wilkes
Barre Real Property. Under the terms of the Purchase Agreement, Kraft paid to
ALCS $9.3 million in consideration for the acquisition of the Wilkes Barre Real
Property and the related personal property.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">San Antonio, Texas.&#160; </font></i>On the Transaction Date, Kraft entered into an Assignment and
Assumption of Lease (the &#147;Assignment and Assumption Agreement&#148;) with ALCS,
pursuant to which ALCS assigned to Kraft, and Kraft assumed, all of ALCS&#146;s
rights under a lease for certain real property located in San Antonio, Texas
(the &#147;San Antonio Real Property&#148;).&#160; In
connection with the Assignment and Assumption Agreement, Kraft also purchased
from ALCS pursuant to a Bill of Sale, dated as of the Transaction Date, &#160;certain personal property located on the San
Antonio Real Property for a purchase price of $6.0 million.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The foregoing descriptions
of the Purchase Agreement, the Assignment and Assumption Agreement and the Bill
of Sale are qualified in their entirety by reference to the complete terms and
conditions of the Purchase Agreement, the Assignment and Assumption Agreement
and the Bill of Sale, as the case may be, which are attached as Exhibits 10.1,
10.2 and 10.3, respectively, to this Current Report on Form 8-K.</font></p>
