 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On March&nbsp;3, 2016, a subsidiary of Acorn Holdings B.V. (&#147;Acorn&#148;), an investor group led by JAB Holding Company, completed its previously
announced acquisition of Keurig Green Mountain, Inc. (&#147;Keurig&#148;). In connection with the acquisition, on March&nbsp;7, 2016, Mondel&#275;z International, Inc. (&#147;we&#148; or &#147;the Company&#148;) exchanged a portion of its equity
ownership in Jacobs Douwe Egberts B.V. (&#147;JDE&#148;) for equity in the new holding company of Keurig, Maple Parent Holdings Corp. (&#147;Maple&#148;). Following the exchange, our percentage share of ownership in JDE has been reduced to 26.5%.
Our percentage share ownership in Maple is 24.24%. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In connection with the exchange, we amended the shareholders&#146; agreement that we previously
entered with a subsidiary of Acorn in relation to JDE (as amended, the &#147;JDE Shareholders&#146; Agreement&#148;) and have entered into a new shareholders&#146; agreement with a subsidiary of Acorn in relation to Maple (the &#147;Maple
Shareholders&#146; Agreement&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>JDE Shareholders&#146; Agreement </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The JDE Shareholders&#146; Agreement does not change significantly the governance rights we had in JDE prior to the amendment. We are now entitled to two seats
on the JDE board, Acorn is entitled to six seats and JDE&#146;s CEO and CFO each are also entitled to a seat. The JDE board will have 10 members, each with one vote. We continue to have certain minority protection rights under the JDE
Shareholders&#146; Agreement, including veto rights over specified decisions relating to the combined business. The Company and Acorn have agreed not to transfer their shares in JDE before July 2018. Thereafter, either party may initiate a public
offering process for its shares, subject, in the case of a proposed transfer by us, to a right of first offer in favor of Acorn. Subject to certain exceptions, each of the parties to the JDE Shareholders&#146; Agreement has agreed not to compete
with the business of JDE for the duration of the JDE Shareholders&#146; Agreement and for two years after such party ceases to be a party to the agreement. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">JDE has agreed to distribute dividends to us and Acorn of at least &#128;175&nbsp;million for each of the years ending December&nbsp;31, 2016 and 2017, and at
least 40% of net operating profit for each subsequent year. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Maple Shareholders&#146; Agreement </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Our governance rights under the Maple Shareholders&#146; Agreement are substantially similar to our rights under the JDE Shareholders&#146; Agreement. We are
entitled to two seats on the Maple board, Acorn is entitled to seven seats and Maple&#146;s CEO is entitled to a seat. In addition, the Maple board may nominate an independent industry expert as an additional director for a total of 11 members. The
Maple board will each have one vote except that one of our two directors will be entitled to two votes. We have certain minority protection rights, including veto rights over specified decisions relating to the combined business. The Company and
Acorn have agreed not to transfer their shares in Maple before July 2018. Thereafter, either party may initiate a public offering process for its shares, subject, in the case of a proposed transfer by us, to a right of first offer in favor of Acorn.
Subject to certain exceptions, each of the parties to the Maple Shareholders&#146; Agreement has agreed not to compete with the business of Maple for the duration of the Maple Shareholders&#146; Agreement and for two years after such party ceases to
be a party to the agreement. </P>

<p Style='page-break-before:always'>
<HR  SIZE="3" style="COLOR:#999999" WIDTH="100%" ALIGN="CENTER">

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Maple has agreed to make pro rata payments of dividends and interest to us and Acorn with respect to each year
totaling at least $132 million, with an increase in dividends for any amount by which 40% of the net operating profit of Maple exceeds $100 million. Any increase in dividends must comply with applicable Maple debt covenants. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The foregoing description of the JDE Shareholders&#146; Agreement and the Maple Shareholders&#146; Agreement is qualified in its entirety by reference to the
complete terms and conditions of the agreements, which we intend to file with our Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2016 or in an amendment to this Current Report on Form 8-K. </P>
