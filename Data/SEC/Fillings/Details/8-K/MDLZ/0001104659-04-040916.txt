<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December&nbsp;22, 2004,
Kraft Foods Aviation, LLC (&#147;Kraft&#148;), a wholly owned indirect subsidiary of
Kraft Foods Inc., entered into an Assignment and Consent with Altria Corporate
Services, Inc. (&#147;ALCS&#148;) and Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation (&#147;Gulfstream&#148;),
pursuant to which ALCS assigned to Kraft, and Kraft assumed from ALCS, ALCS&#146;s
rights and obligations with respect to Aircraft #2 under a Gulfstream G550
Sales Agreement, dated December&nbsp;13, 2004, between ALCS and Gulfstream,
providing for the purchase and outfitting of two G550 aircraft.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The foregoing description of

complete terms and conditions of the Assignment and Consent, which is attached
as Exhibit 10.20 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.</font></p>
