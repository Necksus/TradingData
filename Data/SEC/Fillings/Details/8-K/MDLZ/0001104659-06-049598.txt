<p align="center" style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Kraft
Foods to Sell <i>Minute</i> Rice to Better Focus Its
Brand Portfolio</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">NORTHFIELD, Ill.&#151;(BUSINESS
WIRE)&#151;July 27, 2006&#151;Kraft Foods Inc. (NYSE:KFT), a global leader in branded
food and beverages, announced today that it has agreed to sell its <i>Minute</i> Rice brand and assets to Ebro Puleva, S.A., a leading
rice producer and distributor based in Spain, for approximately $280 million.
The <i>Minute</i> Rice brand had net revenues of
approximately $90 million in 2005.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;Our decision to
sell <i>Minute</i> Rice is part of a broader effort
at Kraft to focus our portfolio,&#148; said Dave Johnson, President, Kraft North
America Commercial. &#147;While <i>Minute</i> Rice is
a great brand, our focus is on businesses in which we believe we have a
sustainable competitive advantage.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The sale includes
the <i>Minute</i> brand, as well as certain
manufacturing equipment.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:20.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The sale is
expected to generate a one-time gain of $0.10 per share and ongoing dilution is
expected to be $0.01 per share, neither of which was included in Kraft&#146;s July
24, 2006 earnings guidance. The gain will be recognized upon closing, which is
subject to regulatory approval.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">About Kraft Foods</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Kraft Foods (NYSE:KFT) is
the world&#146;s second-largest food and beverage company. For more than 100 years,
we&#146;ve helped people around the world eat and live better. Millions of times a
day, in more than 150 countries, consumers reach for their favorite Kraft
brands including <i>Kraft</i> cheeses and dinners, <i>Jacobs</i>, <i>Gevalia</i> and <i>Maxwell House</i> coffees, <i>Oscar Mayer</i>
meats, <i>DiGiorno</i> pizzas, <i>Oreo</i>
cookies, <i>Ritz</i> crackers and chips, <i>Philadelphia</i> cream cheese, <i>Milka</i>

salad dressings and <i>Tang</i> refreshment

<i>South Beach Diet</i> and our growing range
of better-for-you <i>Sensible Solution</i> options to
their shopping baskets, continually expanding their list of Kraft favorites.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For more information on
Kraft Foods, please visit our website at www.kraft.com.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">About Ebro Puleva, S.A.</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Ebro Puleva
(www.ebropuleva.com) is a leading Spanish food group in terms of turnover,
profit, market capitalization and international presence. Ebro Puleva is a

products in the United States.</font></p>
