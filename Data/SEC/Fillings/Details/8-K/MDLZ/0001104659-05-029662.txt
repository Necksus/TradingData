<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(b)&nbsp;On June&nbsp;24,
2005, Kraft Foods Inc. (&#147;Kraft&#148;) and Ms.&nbsp;Holden, President of Global
Marketing&nbsp;&amp; Category Development for Kraft, announced her intention to
leave Kraft and resign her position on Kraft&#146;s Board of Directors, effective June&nbsp;30,
2005.&nbsp; In connection with Ms.&nbsp;Holden&#146;s departure, on June&nbsp;24,
2005, Kraft and Ms.&nbsp;Holden entered into a Separation Agreement and General
Release (the &#147;Separation Agreement&#148;).&#160;
Pursuant to the terms of the Separation Agreement, Ms.&nbsp;Holden will
receive separation payments at her current base salary through June&nbsp;30,
2007 and will be eligible to participate in various Kraft benefit plans
including applicable medical and retirement plans.&#160; Ms.&nbsp;Holden will receive payments in lieu
of awards under the Kraft Management Incentive Plan (&#147;MIP&#148;) and the 2004-2006
Long Term Incentive Plan (&#147;LTIP&#148;), prorated from January&nbsp;1, 2005 through June&nbsp;30,
2005 and January&nbsp;1, 2004 through June&nbsp;30, 2005, respectively.&#160; In consideration for the payments in lieu of
awards under the MIP and LTIP programs, Ms.&nbsp;Holden will agree not to work
for specified competitors through February&nbsp;15, 2007 without the prior
written consent of Kraft.&#160; Ms.&nbsp;Holden
will forfeit her right in the unvested portion of the performance-enhanced
stock option grant issued on June&nbsp;12, 2001 and restricted stock grants
issued in 2003, 2004 and 2005.&#160; Ms.&nbsp;Holden
will receive a payment in lieu of the restricted stock grants based on a pro
rata number of shares in accordance with Kraft&#146;s practice.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Betsy D. Holden (&#147;Holden&#148;) has been employed by

<h4 style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:auto;text-align:center;text-decoration:underline;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">KRAFT FOODS ANNOUNCES MANAGEMENT CHANGES</font></u></b></h4>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">NORTHFIELD, IL, June&nbsp;24, 2005 &#150; Kraft Foods Inc. (NYSE: KFT), a
global leader in branded foods and beverages, announced today that Betsy
Holden, President, Global Marketing&nbsp;&amp; Category Development, has
decided to leave the company and resign her position on the company&#146;s Board of
Directors, effective June&nbsp;30.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Roger Deromedi, Chief Executive Officer of Kraft Foods, said, &#147;Betsy&#146;s
marketing talents and leadership abilities have benefited Kraft throughout her
23-year career. We thank Betsy for her many contributions, including her recent
leadership in establishing the Global Marketing&nbsp;&amp; Category Development
group within our global organization and in guiding our Health&nbsp;&amp;
Wellness initiatives. We wish her all the best in her future endeavors.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Holden has decided to take a 9-12 month sabbatical to spend time with
her teenage children, and then plans to resume her career elsewhere.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">After Holden&#146;s departure, Paula Sneed, 57, formerly Senior Vice
President, Marketing Resources, will become Executive Vice President, Global
Marketing Resources&nbsp;&amp; Initiatives. Sneed and her team will continue to
provide worldwide leadership and oversight of Marketing Resources functions
including </font>Media,
Advertising, Digital Marketing, Kitchens, and Consumer Relations. She has been
with the company for 28 years and has held numerous assignments in brand and
business management, including leadership of the company&#146;s Foodservice and
Desserts divisions in the U.S.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">John Baxter, 45, formerly Senior Vice President, Global Snacks, will
become Executive Vice President, Global Category Development.&#160; In his new role, Baxter and his team will
have responsibility for Kraft&#146;s global consumer sectors, along with Kraft&#146;s
initiative to transform its portfolio in line with consumers&#146; increasing focus
on health and wellness. Baxter has been with Kraft for 16 years and during this
time, has had a wide range of management experiences within Kraft&#146;s
international and North American businesses, including leadership of the
R&amp;D facility in Munich, Germany and responsibility for the Kraft Foods
International Research and Development function.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Baxter
and Sneed will report to Deromedi and become members of the company&#146;s executive
team.</font></p>
