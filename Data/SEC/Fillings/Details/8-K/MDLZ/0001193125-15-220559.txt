 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01.&nbsp;Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The information described below under &#147;Item 2.03. Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation or an Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement of a
Registrant&#148; is hereby incorporated by reference into this Item&nbsp;1.01.</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;2.03.&nbsp;Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation or an
Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement of a Registrant. </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On June&nbsp;11, 2015, we entered into a revolving credit agreement (the
&#147;Agreement&#148;) for a senior unsecured revolving credit facility in an aggregate principal amount of $500 million with JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A. and HSBC Bank USA, National Association. The revolving facility matures on July&nbsp;31, 2015.
</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Under the Agreement, we and certain of our subsidiaries that we may designate may borrow advances up to the aggregate amount of the unused commitments
under the revolving facility on or after June&nbsp;11, 2015 and before the termination of the Agreement. Under the Agreement, we guarantee the obligations of any subsidiary borrower. We have the right, upon certain conditions, to terminate in whole
or reduce ratably in part the unused portions of the respective commitments of the lenders. All committed pro rata borrowings under the revolving facility will bear interest at a variable annual rate based on LIBOR or base rate, at our election,
plus an applicable margin (as determined pursuant to the Agreement). In the case of a borrowing that bears interest based on LIBOR, the rate will be determined by reference to the rating of our long-term senior unsecured debt. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Agreement requires us to maintain a minimum shareholders&#146; equity of not less than $24.6 billion. The Agreement&#146;s definition of minimum
shareholders&#146; equity excludes accumulated other comprehensive income or losses, the cumulative effects of any changes in accounting principles, and any income or losses recognized in connection with the ongoing application of any
&#147;mark-to-market&#148; accounting adopted in respect of pension and other retirement plans. The Agreement also contains customary representations, covenants and events of default. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">We expect to use the Agreement for general corporate purposes, including for working capital purposes. The lenders under the Agreement and their affiliates
have various relationships with us and our subsidiaries involving the provision of financial services, including cash management, investment banking and trust services. In addition, we and some of our subsidiaries have entered into foreign exchange
and other derivatives arrangements with the lenders and their affiliates. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">This description of the Agreement is qualified in its entirety by reference to
the complete terms and conditions of the Agreement, which we will file with our Annual Report on Form 10-Q for the fiscal quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2015. </P>
