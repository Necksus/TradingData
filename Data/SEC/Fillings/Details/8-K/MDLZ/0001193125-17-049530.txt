<P STYLE="margin-top:14pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:7%; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">BOCA RATON, Fla. &#150; Feb. 21, 2017 &#150; At the Consumer Analyst Group of New York (CAGNY) conference today, executives of Mondel&#275;z
International detailed the company&#146;s balanced strategy for both <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">top-</FONT> and bottom-line growth to deliver strong shareholder returns. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:4pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:7%; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Chairman and CEO Irene Rosenfeld highlighted the company&#146;s three-pronged strategy to focus its portfolio, reduce costs and invest for growth to
drive long-term sustainable growth. &#147;We&#146;re one of the few industry players well-positioned to deliver sustainable growth on both the top and bottom lines. We start from a position of strength with the brands, platforms and capabilities
that will create value for our shareholders.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:4pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:7%; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Rosenfeld continued, &#147;We&#146;re experiencing dramatic changes within our industry. This is
causing significant shifts in the drivers of our business &#150; consumer, customers and channels &#150; that are redefining the way our consumers eat, live and shop. We&#146;re constantly evaluating all aspects of our business and working to
capitalize on these shifts to maintain our leadership in the snacking industry over the long term.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:12pt; font-family:ARIAL"><FONT COLOR="#4f2170"><B>Accelerating Growth by Addressing
Key Consumer Trends </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:7%; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Tim Cofer, Chief Growth Officer, provided an update on the company&#146;s strategies to accelerate revenue growth.
