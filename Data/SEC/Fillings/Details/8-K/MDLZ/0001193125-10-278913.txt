series of Notes that may be purchased pursuant to the Tender Offers will not exceed the applicable Maximum Tender Amount. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The
Company also announced preliminary results for the Tender Offers. As of 5:00 p.m., New York City time, on December&nbsp;10, 2010 (the &#147;Early Tender Date&#148;), holders had tendered $1,031,360,000 of 2011 Notes and $944,241,000 of 2012 Notes.
Because holders of each series of Notes validly tendered such Notes in an aggregate principal amount in excess of the applicable Maximum Tender Amount, Kraft Foods will accept an amount of such Notes equal to such Maximum Tender Amount for purchase
and will pay holders of such validly tendered Notes on a pro rata basis among tendering holders of such Notes. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px">&nbsp;</P>
