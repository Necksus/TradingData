<p style="margin-left:0pt;margin-right:0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On

and Governance Committee of the Board.</font></p>

and Exchange Commission Regulation S-K.</font></p>

<h1 align="center" style="font-weight:normal;margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;page-break-after:avoid;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Ajay
Banga Elected to Kraft Foods Board of Directors</font></b></h1>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">NORTHFIELD, Ill. &#151; Jan.
29, 2007 &#151; Kraft Foods Inc. (NYSE: KFT), a global leader in food and beverages,
today announced the election of Ajay Banga to its Board of Directors.&#160; With the addition of Mr. Banga, the company&#146;s
Board increases from nine Directors to 10, six of whom are not employees of
either Kraft or Altria Group, Inc.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:36.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr. Banga, 47, is
Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Citigroup&#146;s Global Consumer Group
International businesses, a position he has held since August 2005. He
previously served as an Executive Vice President of Citigroup&#146;s Global Consumer
Group and President of Citigroup&#146;s Retail Banking North America.&#160; Since joining Citigroup in 1996, Mr. Banga
also has worked as business head for CitiFinancial and the U.S. Consumer Assets
Division, and as division executive for the consumer bank in Central/Eastern
Europe, Middle East, Africa and India.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:36.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr. Banga began
his business career as a management trainee with Nestl&#233; in 1981. He spent the
next 13 years in a variety of assignments spanning sales, marketing and general
management. He later joined PepsiCo in its Restaurants Division and was
instrumental in the launch of Pizza Hut and KFC in India.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0pt 0pt 12.0pt;text-indent:36.0pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;I am very pleased
to welcome Ajay Banga to our Board of Directors,&#148; said Kraft Chairman Louis C.
Camilleri. &#147;His expertise in global business and the consumer packaged goods
industry will be of significant value to Kraft as we work to accelerate our
growth.&#148;</font></p>
