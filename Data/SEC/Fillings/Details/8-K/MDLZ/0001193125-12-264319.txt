</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>KRAFT FOODS INC. TO TRANSFER STOCK LISTING TO NASDAQ </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><I>Company&#146;s Ticker Symbol to Remain &#145;KFT&#146; until Spin-Off of North American Grocery Co. </I></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">NORTHFIELD, Ill. &#150; June&nbsp;8, 2012 &#150; Kraft Foods Inc. today announced that it will transfer its stock exchange listing to The
NASDAQ Global Select Market from the New York Stock Exchange. The company said that its decision to transfer to The NASDAQ Global Select Market, an exchange of The NASDAQ OMX Group Inc. (Nasdaq: NDAQ), will yield greater cost efficiencies, while
providing visibility advantages for the company&#146;s iconic brands. Kraft Foods shares will begin trading on The NASDAQ Stock Market on June&nbsp;26, 2012, and the ticker symbol will remain KFT. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As previously announced, Kraft Foods Inc. will be dividing into two industry-leading public companies before the end of the year: a
high-growth global snacks business called Mondel&#275;z International, Inc., and a high-margin North American grocery business called Kraft Foods Group, Inc. Both of these companies will trade on the NASDAQ exchange. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">At the time of the spin-off later this year, Kraft Foods Inc. will change its name to Mondel&#275;z International, Inc., and the
company&#146;s common stock will trade under the symbol MDLZ. Concurrently, the North American grocery business will be spun off to form a new company, Kraft Foods Group, Inc., whose common stock will trade under the symbol KRFT. The symbol KFT will
be retired at that time. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Until the spin-off, however, the name and stock trading symbol for the company will remain Kraft
