SIZE="2">On December&nbsp;12, 2007, Kraft Foods Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148; or &#147;Kraft&#148;) expects to issue $2,000,000,000 aggregate principal amount of its 6.125% Notes due in 2018 (the &#147;2018 Notes&#148;) and $1,000,000,000 aggregate
principal amount of its 6.875% Notes due in 2038 (the &#147;2038 Notes&#148; and collectively with the 2018 Notes, the &#147;Notes&#148;). The Notes will be issued pursuant to an Indenture (the &#147;Indenture&#148;) dated as of October 17, 2001, by

Company agreed to issue and sell the Notes to the Underwriters. The provisions of an Amended and Restated Underwriting Agreement dated as of December&nbsp;5, 2007 (the &#147;Underwriting Agreement&#148;) are incorporated by reference in the Terms
Agreement. Copies of the Underwriting Agreement and Terms Agreement are attached to this report as Exhibits 1.1 and 1.2, respectively. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Company has

No.&nbsp;333-147829) in connection with the public offering of the Notes. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Notes are subject to certain customary covenants, including limitations on
the Company&#146;s ability, with significant exceptions, to incur debt secured by liens and engage in sale/leaseback transactions. In addition, upon the occurrence of both (i)&nbsp;a change of control of the Company and (ii)&nbsp;a downgrade of the
Notes below an investment grade rating by each of Moody&#146;s Investors Service, Inc., Standard&nbsp;&amp; Poor&#146;s Ratings Services and Fitch Ratings within a specified period, the Company will be required to make an offer to purchase the Notes
of each series at a price equal to 101% of the aggregate principal amount of such series, plus accrued and unpaid interest to the date of repurchase as and to the extent set forth in the Prospectus Supplement. The Company may redeem all, but not
part, of the Notes of each series upon the occurrence of specified tax events as described in the Prospectus Supplement. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Interest on the Notes is payable
semiannually on February&nbsp;1 and August&nbsp;1, commencing August&nbsp;1, 2008, to holders of record on the preceding January&nbsp;15 or July&nbsp;15, as the case may be. Interest on the Notes will be computed on the basis of a 360-day year
consisting of twelve 30-day months. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The 2018 Notes will mature on February&nbsp;1, 2018 and the 2038 Notes will mature on February&nbsp;1, 2038.
