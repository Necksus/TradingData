<B><FONT size=2 face="sans-serif">KRAFT FOODS NOMINATES LUNDGREN AND MESQUITA TO BOARD OF DIRECTORS</FONT></B></P>
<P align="left">
<I><FONT size=2 face="sans-serif">Election to Take Place at Annual Meeting of Shareholders on May 23, 2012</FONT></I></P>
<P align="left">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<FONT size=2 face="sans-serif">NORTHFIELD, Ill. &#150; March 15, 2012 &#150; Kraft Foods (NYSE:KFT) announced today the nominations of Terry J. Lundgren and Jorge S. Mesquita to its Board of Directors. Lundgren, 59, is
Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer of Macy&#146;s, Inc., a premier national retailer, and Mesquita, 50, is Group President, New Business Creation and Innovation for The Procter &amp; Gamble Company, a global marketer of consumer
products.</FONT></P>
<P align="left">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<FONT size=2 face="sans-serif">&#147;Terry and Jorge are outstanding candidates for our Board,&#148; said Chairman and CEO Irene Rosenfeld. &#147;Both are impressive leaders, with outstanding operating and marketing
expertise. In addition, each brings specific skills that will be terrific assets to our business, including Terry&#146;s retail and public company governance experience and Jorge&#146;s consumer products and global business expertise.&#148; Lundgren
has led Macy's since 2003 and has served as a director of the company since 1997. He began his retailing career in 1975 with Bullock's, a division of Federated Department Stores, the predecessor to Macy&#146;s. Over the next 12 years, he held
positions of increasing responsibility in buying, human resources, senior-level store management and merchandising. Lundgren left Federated in 1988 to join Neiman Marcus, where he served as executive vice president and shortly thereafter was named
Chairman and CEO. He returned to Federated in 1994 as Chairman and CEO of the Federated Merchandising Group.</FONT></P>
<P align="left">
<FONT size=2 face="sans-serif">Lundgren received his bachelor&#146;s degree from the University of Arizona.</FONT></P>
<P align="left">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<FONT size=2 face="sans-serif">Mesquita has worked at P&amp;G in various marketing and leadership capacities since joining the company in Venezuela in 1984. Earlier this month, Mesquita assumed a new role at P&amp;G
leading new business creation and innovation. Previously, he led the Global Fabric Care (2007-2012) and Global Home Care (2001-2007) units. Mesquita earned his bachelor&#146;s degree in chemical engineering from the Florida Institute of
Technology.</FONT></P>
<P align="left">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<FONT size=2 face="sans-serif">Kraft Foods also announced today that current directors Ajaypal S. Banga and Richard A. Lerner, M.D., will not stand for re-election.</FONT></P>
<P align="left">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<FONT size=2 face="sans-serif">&#147;Ajay has been a key member of our Board since January 2007,&#148; Rosenfeld said. &#147;He helped guide our company through the spin-off from Altria and set the strategic
direction</FONT></P>

<P align="left">
