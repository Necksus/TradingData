<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:14pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>to Board of Directors </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">DEERFIELD, Ill. &#150; Nov. 24, 2014 &#150; Mondel&#275;z International announced today that it will add Joseph Neubauer to its Board of
Directors. Neubauer, 73, is Chairman of the Board at Aramark, a leading global provider of professional services. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Joe is an
outstanding addition to our Board of highly engaged, world-class leaders,&#148; said Irene Rosenfeld, Chairman and CEO. &#147;He brings tremendous financial acumen, a global perspective and a wealth of experience driving operational excellence in
complex organizations. We welcome his input as we continue to create value for our shareholders.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Neubauer joined Aramark in 1979 as
Executive Vice President of Finance and Development, CFO and a member of the Board of Directors. He was elected President in 1981, CEO in 1983 and Chairman in 1984. He served as Aramark&#146;s Chairman and CEO until 2012. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Prior to Aramark, Neubauer held senior positions with PepsiCo, Inc. from 1971 to 1979, including Senior Vice President of PepsiCo&#146;s
Wilson Sporting Goods Division and Vice President and Treasurer of the parent company, PepsiCo, Inc. From 1965 to 1971, he was with the Chase Manhattan Bank, serving in several capacities from Assistant Treasurer to Vice President of Commercial
Lending. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Neubauer serves on the Board of Directors of Macy&#146;s Inc. He was inducted into the prestigious Horatio Alger Association of
Distinguished Americans in 1994 and currently serves that organization as Immediate Past Chairman. Neubauer received his undergraduate degree from Tufts University and his MBA from the University of Chicago. </P>

of joy in 165 countries, Mondel&#275;z International is a world leader in biscuits, chocolate, gum, candy, coffee and powdered beverages, with billion-dollar brands such as <I>Oreo</I>, <I>LU</I> and <I>Nabisco</I> biscuits; <I>Cadbury, Cadbury
