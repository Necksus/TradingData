 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The information set forth below in Item&nbsp;8.01 is incorporated herein by reference. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Kraft Foods Inc. (the &#147;Company,&#148; &#147;we,&#148; &#147;us,&#148; or &#147;our&#148;) today announced the pricing of $6 billion in aggregate principal amount of notes to be issued by its wholly
owned subsidiary, Kraft Foods Group, Inc. (&#147;Kraft Foods Group&#148;). Kraft Foods Group will issue $1 billion principal amount of its 1.625% Notes due 2015, $1 billion principal amount of its 2.250% Notes due 2017, $2 billion principal amount
of its 3.500% Notes due 2022, and $2 billion principal amount of its 5.000% Notes due 2042 (collectively, the &#147;Notes&#148;). The Notes will be senior unsecured obligations of Kraft Foods Group and will rank equally in right of payment with all
of Kraft Food Group&#146;s existing and future senior unsecured indebtedness, and will be initially guaranteed by us, which guarantee will rank equally in right of payment with all of our existing and future senior unsecured indebtedness. Upon the
consummation of the previously announced proposed spin-off of our North American grocery business to our shareholders (the &#147;Spin-Off&#148;), our guarantee will automatically terminate, and we will automatically be released from our obligations
under the Notes. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">In connection with the issuance of the Notes, we and Kraft Foods Group entered into a Purchase Agreement
dated May&nbsp;30, 2012 (the &#147;Purchase Agreement&#148;) among Kraft Foods Group, as issuer, the Company, as guarantor, and Barclays Capital Inc., Citigroup Global Markets Inc., Goldman, Sachs&nbsp;&amp; Co., J.P. Morgan Securities LLC and RBS
Securities Inc., as representatives of the several initial purchasers named in the Purchase Agreement (the &#147;Initial Purchasers&#148;) pursuant to which Kraft Foods Group agreed to issue and sell the Notes to the Initial Purchasers. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">In connection with the issuance of the Notes, Kraft Foods Group and the Company will enter into an indenture, as supplemented by a first
supplemental indenture (together, the &#147;Indenture&#148;), with Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas, as trustee. The Notes will be subject to certain customary covenants, including limitations on Kraft Food Group&#146;s ability, with significant
exceptions (i)&nbsp;to incur debt secured by liens above a certain threshold, (ii)&nbsp;to engage in certain sale and leaseback transactions above a certain threshold and (iii)&nbsp;to consolidate, merge, convey or transfer its assets substantially
as an entirety. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The issuance of the Notes is expected to close on June&nbsp;4, 2012, subject to customary closing conditions.
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Kraft Foods Group expects to file a copy of the Indenture and the form of global Note for each series of Notes in connection
with its next Form 10 filing in June 2012. </FONT></P>
