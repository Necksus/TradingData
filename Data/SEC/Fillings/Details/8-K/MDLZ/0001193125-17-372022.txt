 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain
Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As previously announced, effective November&nbsp;20, 2017, Irene B. Rosenfeld stepped down
from the position of CEO of Mondel&#275;z International, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) but continues to serve as Chairman of the Board of Directors (the &#147;Board&#148;) for a transition period through March&nbsp;31, 2018, at the end of which she
will retire from the Company and resign from the Board. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On December&nbsp;12, 2017, the Human Resources and Compensation Committee (the &#147;Compensation
Committee&#148;) of the Board approved Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld&#146;s compensation for her service as Chairman for the period from January&nbsp;1, 2018 through March&nbsp;31, 2018. Effective January&nbsp;1, 2018, Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld will receive over the
course of that period $500,000 in salary. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Upon retirement, she will also be eligible to receive continued vesting of her outstanding grants of
performance share units for the 2016-2018 and 2017-2019 performance cycles. The number of shares awarded under each of these grants will be determined by the Compensation Committee based on the Company&#146;s performance on key financial goals for
the relevant performance cycle and will vest on the original vesting dates after each respective performance cycle. Per plan provisions, Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld&#146;s unvested 2016 and 2017 stock option grants will also continue to vest in accordance
with their original vesting schedule and will expire at the end of their full term. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">There is no change to Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld&#146;s compensation for the
period from November&nbsp;20, 2017 through December&nbsp;31, 2017, and she will receive no additional compensation for her service to the Company other than as summarized above. Because she is an employee, Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld is not eligible for any
additional compensation for her service as a director. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The compensation summarized above is subject to Ms.&nbsp;Rosenfeld&#146;s compliance with
restrictive covenants including non-compete, non-solicitation and non-disparagement and confidentiality provisions. </P>
