<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Authorization to $6 Billion; Increases Dividend by 8% </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">DEERFIELD, Ill. &#150; Aug. 7, 2013 &#150; Mondelez International, Inc. announced today that its Board of Directors approved an increase in the company&#146;s share repurchase authorization and an
increase in its quarterly dividend. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;The Board&#146;s actions reflect confidence in our future, our balance sheet

substantial increase in share buybacks creates a highly attractive mix that will deliver superior shareholder returns.&#148; </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Board of Directors approved a significant increase in the company&#146;s current share repurchase program. The company is now
authorized to repurchase up to $6 billion of Mondel&#275;z International Class&nbsp;A Common Stock through 2016, up from $1.2 billion under the previous authorization. The company expects to purchase $1-2 billion annually. Repurchases will be
subject to market conditions and other factors. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Board also declared a regular quarterly dividend of $0.14 per common
share of Class&nbsp;A stock, an increase of $0.01 per share, or 8 percent. The dividend is payable on Oct. 15, 2013, to stockholders of record as of Sept. 30, 2013. This is the first increase in the company&#146;s dividend since 2008. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>About Mondel&#275;z International </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT
