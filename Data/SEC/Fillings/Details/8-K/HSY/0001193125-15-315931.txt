</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>THE HERSHEY COMPANY ANNOUNCES EXPIRATION OF TENDER OFFERS FOR 8.80% DEBENTURES DUE 2021 AND 7.20% DEBENTURES

cash tender offers (the &#147;Tender Offers&#148;) for up to $100 million (the &#147;Tender Cap&#148;) aggregate purchase price of its $100 million outstanding 8.80% Debentures due 2021 (the &#147;2021 Debentures&#148;) and its $250 million

the 2021 Debentures, and (ii)&nbsp;$65,374,000 aggregate principal amount of the 2027 Debentures. The amount of tendered Debentures results in a total purchase price that exceeds the Tender Cap. Based on the Tender Cap and the proration terms of the
Tender Offers, Hershey purchased for cash as part of the previously announced early tender on August&nbsp;26, 2015, $15,285,000 and $56,361,000 aggregate principal amount of the 2021 Debentures and the 2027 Debentures, respectively, resulting in an
aggregate purchase price of $99,999,523 (plus an additional $165,094 in aggregate accrued interest). </P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
