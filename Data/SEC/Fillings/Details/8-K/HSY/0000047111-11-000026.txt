O&#8217;Day, Senior Vice President, Global Operations, and B.&#160;H. Snyder, Senior Vice President, General Counsel and Secretary.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">The independent members of our Board of Directors also approved certain compensation for Mr. West, all as more fully described below.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-STYLE: italic">2011 Annual Incentive Program (AIP) Target Awards.&#160;&#160;</font>On February&#160;21, 2011, the Committee approved 2011 contingent target awards for our named executive officers, excluding Mr. West, and recommended to the independent directors as a group a 2011 contingent target award for Mr.&#160;West, under the annual incentive program (&#8220;AIP&#8221;) of the Company&#8217;s Equity and Incentive Compensation Plan (&#8220;Incentive Plan&#8221;).&#160;&#160;For named executive officers, the final award, if any, will be calculated as the product of the executive officer&#8217;s base salary, applicable target percentage (described below), a corporate performance score (weighted 75%) reflecting the Company&#8217;s achievement in 2011 of certain growth objectives (described below) and
