      K. Arline, Senior Vice President, Chief People Officer, intends to retire as
      of
      December&#160;31, 2007.&#160;&#160;A copy of the Company&#8217;s press release is
      attached hereto and filed as Exhibit 99.1 to this report.</font></div>
    <div><br></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;">Ms.
      Arline first considered retirement under the Company&#8217;s 2005 Early Retirement
      Program (&#8220;ERP&#8221;), but was asked at that time by the Chair of the Compensation and
      Executive Organization Committee (&#8220;Committee&#8221;) of the Company&#8217;s Board of
      Directors to stay on with the Company.&#160;&#160;The Chair of the Committee
      informed Ms. Arline that if she did not retire under the ERP, the Committee
      would consider providing benefits to her in addition to those to which she
      would
      be entitled under the applicable Company benefit plans (&#8220;additional benefits&#8221;)
      upon her actual retirement to compensate her for foregone benefits under the
      ERP.&#160;&#160;Ms. Arline elected not to retire at that time.</font></div>
    <div><br></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;">Prior
      to
      a meeting of the Committee on October 1, 2007, Ms. Arline informed the Committee
      Chair that she was again considering retirement.&#160;&#160;At that meeting, the
      Committee considered, and ultimately decided, to provide the additional benefits
      to Ms. Arline should she elect to retire at the end of 2007.&#160;&#160;A
      Retirement Agreement and General Release (&#8220;Agreement&#8221;), providing for the
      payment in a lump sum of $983,121.00 to approximate the additional benefits
      she
      would have received had she retired under the ERP, was prepared and given to
      Ms.
      Arline to execute if and when she made a final retirement
      decision.&#160;&#160;Ms. Arline indicated her intention to retire by signing and
      delivering the Agreement to the Company on November 11, 2007.</font></div>
