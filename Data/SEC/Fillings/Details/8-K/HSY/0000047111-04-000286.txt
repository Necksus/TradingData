<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On
October 21, 2004, Hershey Foods Corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;) announced that Frank
Cerminara, Senior Vice President, Chief Financial Officer, intends to retire as of
December 31, 2005. Mr. Cerminara&#146;s date of retirement under the terms of the
Company&#146;s pension plan will be January 1, 2006. The Company announced that Mr.
Cerminara would resign his position as Senior Vice President, Chief Financial Officer
effective December 31, 2004 and would continue as an employee of the Company until his
retirement date to assist with the transition of duties to his successor. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Pursuant
to a Retirement Agreement entered into by the Company and Mr. Cerminara as of October 21,
2004, during calendar year 2005 Mr. Cerminara will continue to receive his 2004 base
salary, will be eligible to participate in the Company&#146;s bonus and stock option
programs, will accrue benefit credits under the Company&#146;s Supplemental Executive
Retirement Plan and will participate in pension, health and welfare benefit programs
available generally to Company employees. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The
Company also announced that David J. West, Senior Vice President, Chief Customer Officer,
would become Senior Vice President, Chief Financial Officer, effective January 1, 2005.
From October 25, 2004 until December 31, 2004, Mr. West will hold the office of Senior
Vice President, Finance. Mr. West, who is 41 years old, joined the Company on May 30, 2001
as Vice President, Business Planning and Development. He has since held the positions of
Senior Vice President, Business Planning and Development (elected June 1, 2002); Senior
Vice President, Sales (elected December 1, 2002); and Senior Vice President, Chief
Customer Officer (elected June 18, 2004). Prior to joining the Company, Mr. West was
Senior Vice President Finance, Kraft Foods &#150; Nabisco Biscuit, Confectionery and
Snacks (2001); Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, Nabisco Biscuit Company
(1999); and Vice President, Strategic Planning, Nabisco Holdings Corporation (1998). </FONT></P>

<A NAME=A002></A>
<P ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>Hershey Foods Announces
Changes to Executive Team: <BR>
Cerminara to Retire in 2005; West Named CFO; <BR>
Baldwin Joins Company </B></FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>HERSHEY, Pa., October 21, 2004</B> &#150;
Richard H. Lenny, Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer, Hershey Foods
Corporation (NYSE:HSY), today announced changes to the Hershey Executive Team. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Frank Cerminara, Senior Vice
President, Chief Financial Officer, will retire, effective December 31, 2005. Succeeding
Cerminara will be David J. West, Senior Vice President, Chief Customer Officer. West
begins his new role as CFO on January 1, 2005. Cerminara will work with West during 2005
to ensure an orderly transition. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Christopher J. Baldwin has been named
Senior Vice President, Global Chief Customer Officer, effective October 25, 2004. Baldwin
joins Hershey from Kraft Foods, Inc., where he was National Vice President, Field Sales
and Logistics. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>In making the announcement, Lenny
said, &#147;Frank has been a driving force at Hershey for more than 30 years across many
disciplines. As Chief Financial Officer, Frank has played a key role in helping to shape
today&#146;s successful business and in positioning Hershey for continued future growth.
His leadership has been particularly valuable in ensuring that Hershey&#146;s financial controls
meet today&#146;s higher disclosure and accountability standards. I&#146;m personally grateful for
Frank&#146;s dedication, counsel and commitment. Hershey will continue to benefit from his
experience through the transition.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Commenting on his decision to retire,
Cerminara said, &#147;It has been a privilege for me to work for such an outstanding
company my entire career. I am pleased to be leaving at a time when the company has been
revitalized, is performing superbly in the marketplace and is rewarding our stockholders.
I believe that Hershey has never been better prepared to meet the challenges of the future
than we are today. It has been especially gratifying to have been CFO under the
exceptional leadership provided by Rick Lenny. I have enormous admiration for Rick as an
excellent builder of brands and developer of people.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Dave West joined Hershey Foods in May
2001 as Vice President, Business Planning and Development. He became Senior Vice
President, Business Planning and Development, in 2002 and Senior Vice President, Sales, in
2002, prior to being named to his current position in 2004. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;Dave&#146;s extensive
background in all aspects of finance, coupled with his highly effective leadership of our
selling organization, makes him well prepared for the position of Chief Financial
Officer,&#148; Lenny said. &#147;Dave&#146;s been instrumental in the development of
Hershey&#146;s strategic direction and in its solid execution. His insights, broad
experience within Hershey and inspired leadership will ensure that Hershey continues to
succeed from both a marketplace and financial standpoint.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Prior to joining Hershey Foods, West
was Senior Vice President, Finance, Kraft Foods &#150;&nbsp;Nabisco Biscuit, Confectionery and
Snacks, with responsibility for leading the accounting, reporting and planning function of
Kraft Foods&#146; biscuits, confections and snacks businesses. Prior to Nabisco&#146;s
acquisition by Kraft, West was Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, Nabisco
Biscuit Company. He joined Nabisco in 1987 and held a variety of positions during his
14-year career with the company, including Vice President, Corporate Strategy and Business
Planning; Director, Investment Analysis; Director, General Accounting; and VP/Controller,
Stella D&#146;oro. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Prior to joining Nabisco, he held
positions in finance and cost accounting with Wearever Proctor-Silex and Unisys. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Chris Baldwin will report directly to
Lenny and become a member of the Hershey Executive Team. &#147;Chris is a tremendous
addition to Hershey&#146;s top management team,&#148; Lenny said. &#147;He brings a
stellar track record of accomplishments across multiple customer channels and packaged
consumer goods segments. Throughout his career, Chris has excelled in all essential areas,
including field sales, customer marketing, logistics and organizational development.
Chris&#146; vast experience and energetic, focused leadership will be invaluable as we
continue to build Hershey&#146;s superior selling capabilities and further capitalize on
the immense growth opportunities in the rapidly changing retail environment.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Prior to his current position,
Baldwin served as National Vice President, Sales and Logistics, Kraft Foods; as Vice
President, Sales and Logistics, Nabisco Biscuit; and as Vice President Sales, Nabisco
Biscuit and Snacks. Baldwin held a variety of sales leadership positions with Nabisco
prior to its acquisition by Kraft. Before joining Nabisco, Baldwin worked at Procter &amp;
Gamble in a number of sales positions from 1985 through 1996. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Baldwin received a Bachelor of
Science degree from Siena College, in Loudonville, New York. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Head Left-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<A NAME=A007></A>
<P ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B><U>About Hershey Foods
Corporation</U></B> </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Hershey Foods Corporation (NYSE:HSY)
is a leading snack food company and the largest North American manufacturer of quality
chocolate and non-chocolate confectionery products, with revenues of over $4 billion and
more than 13,000 employees worldwide. The company markets such well-known brands as
<I>Hershey&#146;s, Reese&#146;s</I>, <I>Hershey&#146;s Kisses, Kit Kat, Almond Joy, Mounds, York,
Jolly Rancher, Twizzlers, Ice Breakers</I> and <I>Bubble Yum</I> as well as such innovative new
products as <I>Swoops</I> and <I>Hershey&#146;s S&#146;mores</I>. In addition to its traditional
confectionery products, Hershey offers a range of products specifically developed to
address the nutritional interests of today&#146;s health-conscious consumers. These
products include sugar-free <I>Hershey&#146;s, Reese&#146;s</I> and <I>York</I> candies, as well as
<I>Hershey&#146;s</I> 1 <I>gram Sugar Carb</I> bars for people living a low-carb lifestyle. It also
