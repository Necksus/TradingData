    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">On
February 12, 2008, the Compensation and Executive Organization Committee
(&#8220;Committee&#8221;) of our Board of Directors approved 2008 incentive compensation
awards for certain of the executive officers who were named in the Summary
Compensation Table of our 2007 Proxy Statement (&#8220;named executive
officers&#8221;).&#160;&#160;Three of these officers retired or terminated their
employment with the Company in 2007.&#160;&#160;Richard H. Lenny retired as
Chairman of the Board of Directors, and Marcella K. Arline retired as Chief
People Officer, effective December&#160;31, 2007.&#160;&#160;Thomas K. Hernquist
resigned as an executive officer on December&#160;4, 2007 and discontinued
active employment with the Company on December 31, 2007.&#160;&#160;Of the two
remaining named executive officers, David J. West was elected President of the
Company on October&#160;2, 2007 and Chief Executive Officer effective
December&#160;1, 2007, and J. P. Bilbrey was elected Senior Vice President,
President Hershey North America on December&#160;4, 2007.</font></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">The
independent members of our Board of Directors also approved certain compensation
for Mr. West, as well as the adoption or amendment of certain plans and
agreements, on February&#160;13, 2008, all as more fully described
below.</font></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-STYLE: italic">Special Performance Stock&#160;Unit
(PSU) Retention Award. </font>The Committee approved a special contingent target
award of PSUs (&#8220;special award&#8221;) under our Equity and Incentive Compensation Plan
(&#8220;Incentive Plan&#8221;) for executive officers, including Mr.&#160;Bilbrey, who
received contingent target PSUs for the 2007-2009 performance cycle, and
recommended to the independent directors as a group that Mr. West receive a
special award as he also was a recipient of PSUs for the 2007-2009 performance
cycle. PSU awards are based upon a percentage of the named executive officer&#8217;s
base salary and are earned, if at all, upon the Company&#8217;s achievement of certain
performance objectives over the performance cycle.&#160;&#160;The special award
was made to aid in retention of these executive officers as the potential
retention value of the 2007-2009 PSUs is diminished in light of the Company&#8217;s
2007 financial performance.</font></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br></div>
    <div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">The
special award creates a two-year, 2008-2009 performance cycle under which
eligible executives received contingent target awards equal to two-thirds of the
target value of the PSU award for the 2007-2009 performance
cycle.&#160;&#160;The performance objective for the 2008-2009 performance cycle
is the Company&#8217;s two-year compound annual growth in absolute diluted earnings
per share from operations measured against an internal target. The total
performance score can range from a minimum of 0% to a maximum of
