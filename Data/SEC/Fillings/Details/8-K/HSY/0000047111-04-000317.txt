<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Item 1.01 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Entry into a
Material Definitive Agreement </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On
November 22, 2004, Hershey Foods Corporation (the &#147;Corporation&#148;) entered into a
Stock Purchase Agreement to acquire all of the outstanding stock of Mauna Loa Macadamia
Nut Holdings, Inc., a Delaware corporation, ("Mauna Loa"),  from TSG3 L.P., a Delaware limited
partnership, Darrell F. Askey, Frank G. Bragg,&nbsp;III, Peter J. Burns, Ramona L.  Cappello,
Charles H. Esserman, Charles A. Lynch, Michael L. Mauze, Thomas O&#146;Rourke, J. Gary
Shansby, Yasser Toor and Charles K. H. Young. Mauna Loa operates under the name
Mauna Loa Macadamia Nut Corporation. The purchase price will be $112,400,000, plus assumed debt, which is
estimated to be $17,600,000.
The acquisition is expected to be completed during the fourth quarter of 2004 and is
subject to customary closing conditions and regulatory approval. A copy of the Corporation's
press release dated November 22, 2004 is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this report and is
incorporated herein by reference.</FONT></P>

<A NAME=A002></A>
<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>HERSHEY FOODS
CORPORATION TO ACQUIRE MAUNA LOA,<BR> THE LEADER IN MACADAMIA
SNACKS </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>HERSHEY, Pa., November 22, 2004
&#150;</B> Hershey Foods Corporation (NYSE: HSY), a leading snack food marketer and the
largest North American confectionery company, today announced it has entered into an
agreement to acquire Mauna Loa Macadamia Nut Corporation from The Shansby Group, a private
equity investment firm. Mauna Loa is the leading processor and marketer of macadamia
snacks, with annual sales of approximately $80 million and a 40-percent share in macadamia
nuts. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;Mauna Loa&#146;s portfolio of
premium macadamia nuts, nut-based confections and cookies is a great addition to
Hershey&#146;s stable of iconic brands,&#148; said Richard H. Lenny, Chairman, President
and Chief Executive Officer, Hershey Foods Corporation. &#147;Its superior brand equity,
leadership in an on-trend segment and proven extendibility will strengthen our presence in
the broader snack market. We&#146;re excited about the opportunity to unlock Mauna
Loa&#146;s immense growth potential as we leverage Hershey&#146;s expertise in product
innovation, nut processing, logistics and selling.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;This is a tremendous
opportunity for Mauna Loa,&#148; said Darrell F. Askey, President, Mauna Loa Macadamia Nut
Corporation. &#147;We&#146;re proud of Mauna Loa&#146;s performance and our strong sales
growth shows the momentum and significant marketplace potential of our brand. Now, with
the addition of Hershey&#146;s expanded channel and marketing expertise, we can take Mauna
Loa&#146;s portfolio of premium macadamia nut offerings to new heights.&#148; </FONT></P>


<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The acquisition is expected to be
completed during the fourth quarter of 2004 and is subject to the customary closing
conditions and regulatory approval. It is projected to be slightly accretive in the first
12 months. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Head Major Left Bold-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<A NAME=A005></A>
<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>About Hershey Foods
Corporation </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Hershey Foods Corporation (NYSE: HSY)
is a leading snack food company, the largest North American manufacturer of quality
chocolate and non-chocolate confectionery products, and a leading processor of nuts. With
revenues of over $4 billion and more than 13,000 employees worldwide, Hershey&#146;s
markets such well-known brands as <I>Hershey&#146;s, Reese&#146;s, Hershey&#146;s Kisses, Kit
Kat, Almond Joy, Mounds, York, Jolly Rancher, Twizzlers</I>, and <I>Ice Breakers</I> as well as
innovative new products such as <I>Swoops</I> and <I>Hershey&#146;s S&#146;mores</I>. In addition to its
traditional confectionery products, Hershey offers a range of products specifically
developed to address the nutritional interests of today&#146;s health-conscious consumer.
These products include sugar-free <I>Hershey&#146;s, Reese&#146;s</I> and <I>York</I> candies, as well
as <I>Hershey&#146;s SmartZone</I> bars for people seeking balanced nutrition. It also markets
<I>Hershey&#146;s</I> cocoa, <I>Hershey&#146;s</I> syrup and other branded baking ingredients, toppings
and beverages. Visit us at <U>www.hersheynewsroom.com</U>. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Head Major Left Bold-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<A NAME=A006></A>
<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>About The Shansby Group </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The Shansby Group (TSG) is a leading
private equity firm focused exclusively on investing in branded consumer product
companies. With over $800 million in capital under management, TSG has invested in such
brands as <I>Terra Chips, glaceau vitaminwater, Don Miguel, Famous Amos, MET-Rx, Mauna Loa</I>
and others. The firm works closely with management teams to build leading companies and
strong brands. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Head Major Left Bold-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<A NAME=A007></A>
<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>About Mauna Loa </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Based in Hilo, Hawaii, Mauna Loa is a
