<A NAME=A002></A>
<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Hershey Foods
Announces Price Increases </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>HERSHEY, Pa., December 16, 2004
&#151; </B>Hershey Foods Corporation (NYSE:HSY) today announced an increase in the
wholesale prices of approximately half of its domestic confectionery line. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>A weighted average 5.8% increase on
the company&#146;s standard bar, king-size bar, 6-pack and vending lines is effective
immediately. During the eight-week period ending February 14, 2005, however, existing
customers may, based on their historic order patterns, order and take delivery of up to
12-weeks of inventory of these items at current prices. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>A weighted average 4.1% increase on
packaged chocolates will be effective February 14, 2005. During the six-week period from
February 14 to March 28, 2005, however, customers may, based on their historic order
patterns, order and take delivery of up to 10 weeks of inventory of these items at current
prices. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The changes approximate a 3% price
increase over Hershey&#146;s entire domestic product line and will help offset increases
in areas of the company&#146;s input costs, including raw and packaging materials, fuel,
utilities, transportation and employee benefits. </FONT></P>

<A NAME=A003></A>
<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>About Hershey Foods
Corporation </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Hershey Foods Corporation (NYSE: HSY)
is a leading snack food company and the largest North American manufacturer of quality
chocolate and non-chocolate confectionery products. With revenues of over $4 billion and
more than 13,000 employees worldwide, Hershey Foods' markets such well-known brands as
<I>Hershey&#146;s</I>, <I>Reese&#146;s</I>, <I>Hershey&#146;s Kisses</I>, <I>Kit Kat</I>,
<I>Almond Joy, Mounds, York, Jolly Rancher, Twizzlers,</I> and<I> Ice</I> <I>Breakers</I>
as well as innovative new products such as <I>Swoops</I> and <I>Hershey&#146;s
S&#146;mores</I>. In addition to its traditional confectionery products, Hershey Foods offers a
range of products specifically developed to address the nutritional interests of
today&#146;s health-conscious consumer. These products include sugar-free
<I>Hershey&#146;s, Reese&#146;s</I> and <I>York</I> candies, as well as <I>Hershey&#146;s
SmartZone </I>bars for people seeking balanced nutrition. It also markets <I>Hershey&#146;s</I>
cocoa, <I>Hershey&#146;s</I> syrup and other branded baking ingredients, toppings and
beverages. Visit us at <U>www.hersheynewsroom.com</U>. </FONT></P>
