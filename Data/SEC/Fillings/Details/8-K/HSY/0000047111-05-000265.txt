<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Item 1.01 Entry Into a
Material Definitive Agreement </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>On December 6, 2005, the Board of
Directors of The Hershey Company approved an increase in non-employee director
compensation effective January 1, 2006. The increase was approved following a review of
competitive data that disclosed the need to adjust director compensation upward to be at
the mid-point of compensation paid to directors at a peer group of food, beverage and
consumer packaged goods companies the Board uses for benchmarking non-employee director
compensation. </FONT></P>
