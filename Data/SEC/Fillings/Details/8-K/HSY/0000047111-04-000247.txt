<P ALIGN=RIGHT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>EXHIBIT 99.1 </FONT></P>

<A NAME=A006></A>
<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>HERSHEY MEXICO, A
SUBSIDIARY OF HERSHEY FOODS CORPORATION,<BR>TO ACQUIRE GRUPO LORENA, <BR>A LEADING MEXICAN
CONFECTIONERY COMPANY </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>HERSHEY, Pa., August 17, 2004</B>
&#150; Hershey Mexico, a subsidiary of Hershey Foods Corporation (NYSE: HSY) and one of
the leading companies manufacturing and selling chocolate, confectionery and flavored milk
products in Mexico, today announced it has entered into an agreement to acquire Grupo
Lorena, one of Mexico&#146;s top confectionery companies. Grupo Lorena, which had sales of
more than $30 million in 2003, is a leader in the spicy candy market in Mexico with its
<I>Pel&oacute;n Pelo Rico</I>&reg; brand. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;Grupo Lorena&#146;s solid
brands in key consumer segments will strengthen our leadership position in the Mexican
market,&#148; said Juan-Carlos Zayas, Director of Marketing, Hershey Mexico. &#147;This
portfolio will broaden our offerings for Mexican consumers, especially as its strength in
the youth market complements our established success in the adult segment. Its
<I>Pel&oacute;n Pelo Rico</I><SUP>&reg;</SUP> brand is a great entry to the popular spicy
candy segment for Hershey Mexico. Equally important, Grupo Lorena&#146;s commitment to
product quality and innovation is a perfect fit with Hershey&#146;s reputation for
manufacturing products that meet consumer expectations for great taste and top
quality.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;Grupo Lorena is a great
addition to Hershey Foods&#146; portfolio of excellent brands,&#148; said Richard H.
Lenny, Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer, Hershey Foods Corporation.
&#147;It significantly increases our existing business in Mexico and supports our strategy
of profitably building scale in the key North American market. Lorena also will deliver
expanded retail channel and customer growth opportunities for Hershey, both in Mexico and
the United States. We look forward to building Grupo Lorena&#146;s brands with the
Hispanic consumer and leveraging its capabilities to introduce innovative Hershey products
to the high-growth Hispanic segment.&#148; </FONT></P>


<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>As part of Hershey Foods&#146;
strategy to tap into the fast-growing U.S. Latino market, the company recently announced a
multi-year partnership with Mexican-born Thal&iacute;a Sodi, the Latin singer and actress
popular with millions of fans around the world. The partnership includes sponsorship of
Thal&iacute;a&#146;s 2004 U.S. tour, a Spanish-language advertising campaign, consumer and
retail promotions, and a new, co-branded Thal&iacute;a line of Hershey products designed
to appeal to the Latino community. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;Hershey, with its longstanding
commitment to manufacturing top-quality and great-tasting products, is a perfect match for
Grupo Lorena,&#148; said Fernando Topete, President, Grupo Lorena. &#147;Whether in
Mexico, the United States or Latin America, our consumers will be able to enjoy all their
favorite Lorena products with complete assurance that they meet the highest standards for
quality.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The acquisition is expected to be
completed during the fourth quarter of 2004 and is subject to the customary closing
conditions and regulatory approval. The acquisition is expected to be slightly accretive
in the first 12 months. Terms of the agreement were not disclosed. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Head Major Left Bold-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<A NAME=A008></A>
<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>About Hershey Foods
Corporation </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Hershey Foods Corporation (NYSE: HSY)
is a leading snack food company and the largest North American manufacturer of quality
chocolate and non-chocolate confectionery products, with revenues of over $4 billion and
more than 13,000 employees worldwide. The company markets such well-known brands as
<I>Hershey&#146;s</I>, <I>Reese&#146;s</I>, <I>Hershey&#146;s Kisses</I>, <I>Kit Kat</I>,
<I>Almond Joy, Mounds, York, Jolly Rancher, Twizzlers, Ice</I> <I>Breakers</I> and
<I>Bubble Yum</I> as well as innovative new products such as <I>Swoops</I> and
<I>Hershey&#146;s S&#146;mores</I>. In addition to its traditional confectionery products,
Hershey offers a range of products specifically developed to address the nutritional
interests of today&#146;s health-conscious consumer. These products include sugar-free<I>
Hershey&#146;s, Reese&#146;s</I> and <I>York</I> candies, as well as <I>Hershey&#146;s 1
gram Sugar Carb </I>bars for people living a low-carb lifestyle. It also markets
<I>Hershey&#146;s </I>cocoa, <I>Hershey&#146;s </I>syrup and other branded baking
ingredients, toppings and beverages. Visit us at <U>www.hersheynewsroom.com.</U> </FONT></P>
