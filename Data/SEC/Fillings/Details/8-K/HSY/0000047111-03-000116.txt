<P  ALIGN=CENTER><FONT  FACE="Times New Roman,  Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B> John M.
Pietruski   to   Retire   from   Hershey    Foods    Corporation's    Board   of
Directors</B></FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>HERSHEY,  Pa., April 22,
2003</B> - Hershey Foods  Corporation today announced that John M. Pietruski has
retired from its Board of Directors  effective  April 22, 2003. A Hershey  Foods
Director  since 1987,  he chaired  the  Committee  on  Directors  and  Corporate
Governance and was a member of the Executive Committee. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Pietruski also is Chairman
of the Board of Texas Biotechnology Corporation, Houston, Texas, a
pharmaceutical research and development company. He is a director of FirstEnergy
Corporation and Professional Detailing Inc. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times  New  Roman,  Times,  Serif"  SIZE=2>In   announcing  the
retirement,  Richard H. Lenny,  Chairman,  President and Chief Executive Officer
said,  "Hershey has benefited  greatly from Jack's vast  experience,  discerning
questions  and  solid  advice  over the  past 16  years.  We  thank  him for his
dedicated service to the Company."</FONT></P>

<P ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B><U>About Hershey
Foods Corporation</U></B><BR>
Hershey Foods Corporation (NYSE:HSY)
is the leading North American manufacturer of quality chocolate and
non-chocolate confectionery and chocolate-related grocery products. Some of the
company&#146;s most popular products include HERSHEY<SUP>&#174;</SUP>&#146;S
chocolate and chocolate with almonds bars, HERSHEY<SUP>&#174;</SUP>&#146;S
KISSES<SUP>&#174; </SUP>chocolates, REESE&#146;S<SUP>&#174;</SUP> peanut butter
cups, JOLLY RANCHER<SUP>&#174;</SUP> and TWIZZLERS<SUP>&#174;</SUP> candies,
HERSHEY<SUP>&#174;</SUP>&#146;S cocoa, and HERSHEY<SUP>&#174;</SUP>&#146;S
syrup. The company also is a market leader in the gum and mint category with
such well-known brands as ICE BREAKERS<SUP>&#174;</SUP>, BREATH
SAVERS<SUP>&#174;</SUP>, and BUBBLE YUM<SUP>&#174;</SUP>. </FONT></P>

<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Harriet
Edelman and Marie J. Toulantis Elected to<BR>
Hershey Foods Corporation&#146;s Board of Directors</FONT></H1>

<P><FONT  FACE="Times New Roman,  Times, Serif"  SIZE=2><B>HERSHEY,  Pa., April 22,
2003</B> - Hershey Foods  Corporation  today  announced the election to its Board of
Directors  of Harriet  Edelman,  Senior  Vice  President  and Chief  Information
Officer,  Avon Products,  Inc., and Marie J. Toulantis,  Chief Executive Officer,
Barnes & Noble.com, effective April 22, 2003.</FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>In announcing the
elections, Richard H. Lenny, Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer
said, &#147;We&#146;re most pleased to have both Harriet and Marie join the
Hershey Foods Board of Directors. Their vast experience in the consumer goods
industry is of great value as we continue to build the Hershey portfolio of
leading brands.&#148; </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Harriet Edelman currently
serves as Senior Vice President and Chief Information Officer, Avon Products,
Inc., with responsibility for Avon&#146;s information technology strategy and
operations globally. Avon&#146;s 1,400 Information Technology Associates around
the world deliver technology infrastructure and solutions to serve the
company&#146;s 3.9 million sales Representatives and operations in 143
countries. Edelman has held leadership positions in marketing, sales, supply
chain, customer service, and planning and information technology, with U.S. and
international responsibilities. She is a member of the Operating Committee and
has served as an officer of the company since 1987. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Edelman holds a
master&#146;s of business administration in marketing from Fordham&#146;s
Graduate Business School and a bachelor&#146;s degree in music from Bucknell
University. She serves on the Board of Directors of the Blair Corporation, the
Direct Selling Association, the Police Athletic League and the Institute of
Women&#146;s Policy Research. </FONT></P>

<P ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>   - more -</FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Marie J. Toulantis
currently serves as Chief Executive Officer of Barnes &amp; Noble.com, the
publicly-traded Internet commerce company. Toulantis joined Barnes &amp;
Noble.com as Chief Financial Officer in 1999 and oversaw its successful initial
public offering (IPO), which raised nearly $500 million in the largest Internet
IPO to be completed at that time. She was appointed President and Chief
Operating Officer in 2001, and under her leadership Barnes &amp; Noble.com has
become one of the top-five e-commerce Web sites, attracting approximately 10
million unique visitors per month. Toulantis joined Barnes &amp; Noble, Inc., in
1997 as Executive Vice President with responsibility for Finance and later was
appointed Chief Financial Officer. Prior to Barnes &amp; Noble, Inc., she served
as Senior Vice President at the Chase Manhattan Bank where she oversaw the
bank&#146;s relationship with mid-size companies in Manhattan. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Toulantis holds a
bachelor&#146;s of business administration in marketing from Pace
University&#146;s Lubin School of Business. </FONT></P>
