SECTION 5 - CORPORATE GOVERNANCE AND MANAGEMENT

ITEM 5.02  DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL  OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS;
           APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS

On May 19, 2006,  ALLETE,  Inc. (ALLETE or Company)  announced James K. Vizanko,
it's Senior Vice  President and Chief  Financial  Officer,  will retire from the
Company in early fall 2006 to assume a faculty  position  at the  University  of
Minnesota, Duluth  and to devote more time to his family and personal interests.
Mr.  Vizanko has been ALLETE's  Chief  Financial  Officer since August 2001. The
Company has initiated efforts to name a new Chief Financial Officer.
