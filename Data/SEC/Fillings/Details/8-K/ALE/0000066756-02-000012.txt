ITEM 5.  OTHER EVENTS.

Reference is made to the 2000 Form 10-K of ALLETE, Inc. (ALLETE) for background
information on the following update.


Ref. Page 37 - Executive Officers of the Registrant
Ref. 8-K dated and filed August 29, 2001

On January 23, 2003 the ALLETE Board of Directors  elected  David Gartzke to the
positions of chairman of the board and chief  executive  officer of ALLETE.  Mr.
Gartzke retains his current title as president of ALLETE.

Arend Sandbulte stepped down from the position of chairman of the board and will
be  retiring  from the  board in May 2002  pursuant  to the  board's  retirement
policy.
